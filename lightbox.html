Lightbox

Illuminated Signs are suitable for indoor and outdoor use, and are a great way to differentiate your business from your competitors and is one of the most eye-catching forms of advertising. They are the most widely used form of signage due to its simplicity and cost effectiveness.

We custom fabricate every lightbox in our warehouse to any size, thickness and colour you desire. All light boxes are made with durable aluminium single and double sided box signs complete with light fittings, Perspex Panels and Vinyl Graphics. Your light box can be a traditional backlit sign box or an ultra thin edge-lit light box (or light panel)

During the final stages of manufacturing, our team of electricians wire the box up to ensure that the lightbox is safe before the installation process.

* Standard size of 2440mm x 500mm including high quality digital print from $780.
